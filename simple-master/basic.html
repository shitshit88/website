AG MAC Import Export Co.

...is established in 2012 in Columbus, Ohio by three partners Josif, Marjan and Jimmy that had a vision to unite the best Macedonian products and represents them to the US market. We have organized ourselves in order to provide products from The Republic of Macedonia with a passion to help stimulate economic development in Macedonia while sharing their great product with our American friends and families from all backgrounds. We have selected several unique wines from the best wine producers which has become the cornerstone of the company. Never the less other products followed to share with our North American friends on the following pages. Please revisit from time to time to see what new products we may be offering. Our aim will be to continue to import and represent products and companies that have high quality for the best value. The goal is to unite as much companies and products as we can in our portfolio.
