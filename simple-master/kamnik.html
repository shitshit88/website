Chateau Kamnik winery

It all started as a very personal fascination with the wine-making art that the founder, a wine enthusiast from Skopje, has always had; everything else came as a completely natural nowadays expression of the authentic Macedonian wine-making traditions. From collecting wines from all over the globe, through the first vines planted and their transformation into vineyards, through the foundations of the boutique winery resembling a castle, and the first barriques in it, to the first wines bottled, and then many more travelling around the world, some winning great awards, this man shared his vision with a team of excellent people, and turned it into a quest called Kamnik Wines, our creations combining past and future, now; keeping our story alive.

PRODUCTS    

PINOT GRIGIO The wine is produced 100% from the Pinot Grigio variety grown in the vineyards surrounding the Kamnik winery. It is characterized by a distinct haylike pale color with green hues. This dry and fresh wine is rich in its appealing fruity aroma that reminds of banana puree with citrus subtones. The taste is full-bodied, varietal, with a typical freshness, mineral subtones and with a pleasant and harmonious aftertaste. It is best served chilled at 8-10°С, combined with fresh sea fish, salmon startеrs, pasta in white sauce and chicken meat.

SAUVIGNON BLANC Estate bottled wine, produced 100% from the Sauvignon Blanc variety. This wine has a specific green-yellow color and crystal clearness. The nose is varietal and typical for the variety, revealing tender vegetative notes as a blend of citrus and tropical fruits. The taste is full-bodied, with a typical freshness, mineral subtones and with a pleasant and harmonious aftertaste. Enjoy this wine chilled at 8-10оС, with fresh seafood, salmon starters, green salads and chicken meat specialties.

TEMJANIKA 2015 An aromatic dry white wine made 100 % from the Temjanika grape variety. The wine is marked with a brilliant, haylike, pale gold color. What makes it particularly elegant is the perfect synergy of the primary fruity aromas complemented with secondary spicy hints. The wine is characterized by a fresh and intense note of citrus fruits with discrete aromas of elder and coriander. The wine is medium-bodied and it offers a sophisticated and harmonious freshness; the aftertaste provides a pleasant sensation which makes this wine very smooth in the mouth. Enjoy this wine as an aperitif or combined with light starters, salads оr chicken meat specialties. It is also a perfect match with assorted cookies and fruit pies. Recommended serving temperature: chilled to 8-10°C.

CHARDONNAY SINGLE VINEYARD 2015 Premium Dry White Wine This estate bottled wine, produced 100% from the Chardonnay variety, is defined by a crystal clearness and a remarkable yellow-green color. The discreet aromas of pineapple and citrus fruits that are complemented with flint undertones, make this wine especially attractive and modern. The taste is full-bodied, sophisticated and complex; the dominant citrus freshness and the mineral undertones provide the long and enjoyable aftertaste. This wine is perfectly combined with pumpkin ravioli, pasta, white meat and assorted cheese. Recommended serving temperature: 8-12°С.

CHARDONNAY Barrel Fermented 2014 Estate bottled wine, produced 100% from the Chardonnay variety. The uniqueness of this Chardonnay results from the barrel fermentation with extended contact of the wine on the fine lees in French oak barriques, the so called “Sur Lie” method, as well as from the 9-month ageing in these barriques. This wine is characterized by a yellow-green color and crystal clearness. The nose is complex, splendid and elegant, with discrete tropical fruit aromas. The taste is creamy, with a buttery and biscuit texture, and with a fruity aftertaste of melon and pear. The wine has a remarkable fullness, harmony and roundness, and a great potential for ageing in bottle. Enjoy this wine combined with all sorts of white meat, seafood, fish, pasta and selected cheeses. Recommended serving temperature: chilled at 10-12oC.

GOLDEN TEMJANIKA 2015 premium semi-dry white wine Golden Temjanika is a premium semi-dry white wine produced from selected Temjanika grapes. The characteristics and the attractiveness of this wine are emphasized with the tender flakes of genuine 23-carat gold added in the wine itself. Golden Temjanika, except for its ultramodern, tenderly glittering appearance, is characterized with complex fruity aromas of dry apricots and orange peel, complemented with spicy undertones. The taste is full-bodied, well balanced, with a harmonious freshness, and with a fruity and spicy aftertaste. The gold flakes in this wine are edible and safe for consumption and gastronomic use, and they do not affect the taste and the nose of the wine. Golden Temjanika is a wine inspired by the magnificence of the ordinary moments we share with others. The visual extravagance and the touch of luxury of this wine send out a message reminding us of the beauty and preciousness of enjoying life’s simple moments. Enjoy this wine as an appetitive, combined with light starters, with assorted cookies, apple pie or poached pears. Recommended serving temperature: 8-10оС.

WINEMAKERS’ SELECTION 2014 A dry red wine produced as a blend from carefully selected grapes in our vineyards of the distinguished varieties: Vranec (40%), Merlot (40%) and Carmenere (20%). The wine has a ruby red color. The aroma is complex; the dominant fruity-spicy character reveals ripe morello cherries complemented with discrete vanilla sub-tones. The taste is full-bodied, with a velvety structure, soft tannins and with a long aftertaste. Combine this wine with selected strong cheeses, Italian sausages and various meat specialties. Recommended serving temperature: 14-16°С.

MONTEPULCIANO 2013 Estate bottled dry red wine with controlled and guaranteed origin, produced from the Montepulciano variety; crop reduction applied. This wine is defined by a deep ruby red color that is emphasized through the vinification with 3% of grapes of the Vranec grape variety. The nose is intense, with aromas of ripe blackberries and wild cherries. The taste is remarkable; at the very beginning it is introducing an explosion of combined tastes of cappuccino, tobacco, and fine strings of smoke, finishing with a jammy aftertaste. The harmonic acids and the soft velvety tannins provide this wine with a several-year ageing capacity. Best served at 16-17°C, combined with red meat specialties, foie gras with onion jam, or with selected strong cheeses.

CUVÉE DE PRESTIGE 2013 Estate bottled dry red wine with a controlled and guaranteed origin, produced by vinification of delicately selected grapes of the Merlot (69%), Cabernet Sauvignon (18%) and of the Vranec (13%) grape varieties. The wine is characterized by an intense ruby red color, an appealing aroma of ripe dark berries and discrete vegetative notes complemented with dark chocolate hints. The complexity of this Cuvée results from the several-month ageing in new and partly used 225L oak barrels. The taste is full-bodied, with a high content of extract, harmonic acids and soft velvety tannins that provide this wine with ageing capacity of several years. Enjoy this wine with strong cheeses, meat and game specialties, and with spicy dishes as well. Decanting prior serving recommended. Best served at: 14-16°С.

TEN BARRELS SYRAH Reserva 2013 This wine is made from Syrah grapes from the vineyards surrounding the Kamnik winery. It is characterized by a vivacious and deep ruby red color and crystal clearness. The bouquet is rich in dark berries, which are typical for the variety, and with discreet spicy tones of clove, dark chocolate, vanilla and liqorice. The complexity of the wine is revealed in its elegant and full-bodied taste with excellent structure, soft velvety tannins and well integrated oak aromas finishing with a long aftertaste. The splendor of this syrah comes as a result of the 23 months of ageing in new American and French oak barriques. Ten Barrels Syrah Reserva 2013 is perfectly combined with meat specialties, especially with game in spicy sauces. Recommended serving temperature: 16-18°C. The wine has a bottle-ageing capacity of several years.

Single Vineyard Reserva 2011 A dry red wine produced 100% from delicately selected Merlot grapes. The wine has a remarkable deep ruby red color. The nose is complex, with a fruity character revealing dark cherries, plum preserve, complemented with spicy hints of vanilla, smoke and roasted coffee. The abundant aroma comes as a result of the 33 months of ageing in new American and French oak barriques. This wine has a rich and full-bodied taste, with soft velvety tannins that provide the long and persistent aftertaste of fruit and chocolate. Bottle ageing for an extended period, under appropriate ambient conditions, will add even more complexity and splendor to this wine. Pairing: A perfect match with meat specialties, especially game in piquant sauces. Recommended serving temperature 15-18°C. Decanting prior serving recommended.

TEN BARRELS CABERNET SAUVIGNON Reserva 2011 This premium red wine, produced from the Cabernet Sauvignon variety, is defined by a deep ruby red color with dark red hues. A genuine reserva wine with a recognizable, ripe, abundant nose with fruity and spicy character. This wine unveils berries combined with liquorice, dark chocolate, roasted coffee and mint, complemented with vanilla and spicy tones. The complexity of this wine results from the 31-month ageing process in new French and American oak barriques. The taste is full-bodied, harmonious, with a solid structure, impressive and strong tannins, and with a long and pleasant aftertaste that marks this wine with a specific charm. This wine is perfectly combined with meat specialties, especially with beef steak in a green pepper sauce or game in a spicy sauce. Recommended serving temperature: 16-18°С.

TERROIR VRANEC Grand Reserva 2012 A dry red wine produced 100% from Vranec variety grapes that were let to partially dry on the vines. The density is typical for this variety; it has excellent vivacity and nice viscosity. The wine is defined by a dark ruby red color; the fruity and spicy nose unveils a fusion of ripe blackberries, blueberries and plums, with a discrete hint of mint. The 22-month ageing process in new American oak barriqes ensures obtaining an exceptionally full-bodied and intense taste of red berries jam, and a long and persistent chocolate aftertaste. The harmonic acids, the soft velvety tannins and the complex bouquet with a delicate structure provide this wine with a long ageing potential. If aged for an extended period, under appropriate ambient conditions, this wine will show even more complexity and splendor. Recommended serving temperature: 16-18°С Pairing suggestions: with red meat specialties, game in a piquant sauce, or strong cheeses. Decanting prior serving recommended.

SIGNATURE MERLOT Grand Reserva 2012 A special semi-dry red wine produced from 100% carefully selected grapes of the Merlot variety. The wine is characterized by a deep ruby red color with dark purple hues. The curiosity about this wine is the natural crop reduction resulting in partial dehydration of the grapes on the vine itself. The 23-months ageing process that takes place in new American and French oak barriques results in an intense, ripe and complex nose of red berries purée, discrete vanilla and hues of menthol and clove. The taste is rich, full-bodied and exceptionally ripe, with a soft tannin structure and harmonic acids, and with a long-lasting fruit-&-chocolate aftertaste. The extended bottle ageing period, under appropriate conditions, will add even more complexity to this wine. This wine is perfectly combined with red meat specialties, game in piquant sauce, strong cheeses. Recommended serving temperature: 15-18°C. Decanting prior serving recommended.

CABERNET SAUVIGNON Late Harvest 2009 A premium dessert red wine produced from selected late harvest grapes of the Cabernet Sauvignon variety. The wine is crystal clear, defined by a tender ruby red color. The intense fruit-and-spice nose reveals a sweet mixture of berries complemented with hints of green peppers, as a feature of the variety. The taste is full-bodied, harmonic and highly extractive, with pleasant acids that make this wine well balanced, with a long aftertaste. Serve CABERNET SAUVIGNON Late Harvest 2009 alone, or as a dessert combined with selected strong cheeses or dark chocolate cake. Recommended serving temperature: 13-15°С.

ROSÉ SEXY 2015 Premium Dry Rose Wine This rosе wine is created by blending several grape varieties, 48% Sangiovese, 20% Motepulciano, 12% Arinarnoa and 20% Cabernet Sauvignon through application of the so-called “saignée” method. This method enables a few hours of a direct contact of the grape juice with the grape skins, resulting in an outstanding bright rose color of the wine. The nose is remarkable, with forest berries aroma; the dominant raspberry and strawberry notes are complemented with discrete sweet cherry hints. The taste is medium-bodied, fruity, with impressive freshness and balance. Enjoy this rose wine as an aperitif, or with fresh fruit salads, chilled at 8 -10°C.
